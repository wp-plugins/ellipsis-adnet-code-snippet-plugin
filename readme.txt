This plugin will help you embed ellipsis AdNet Banner, Text and Lead Capture Ads within your blog posts.

== Description ==

ellipsis AdNet is a network of trusted publishers and merchants that enables simple, safe and transparent partnership for the purpose of advertising over the Internet. AdNet cuts the "middle man" and helps connect publishers and merchants with full transparency. This plugin will help you embed ellipsis AdNet Banner, Text and Lead Capture Ads within your blog posts.

== Installation ==

1. Download and unzip the plugin to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Use [adnet key='<your Ad's ellipsis AdNet key>' /] in your posts where ever you want to show that specific ellipsis AdNet Ad.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I find my Ad's key? =

Simply click on the "View Code" button against your Ad's contract and you will see the key associated with that particular Ad there.
